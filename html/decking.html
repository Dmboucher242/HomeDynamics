Painted Decks

The cheapest way to transform your deck is with a fresh coat of paint! We make sure to sand down old paint and any imperfections, thoroughly wash, and seal with the paint color of your choice. We ensure that you will love this nice quick repair!

Stained Decks

If you're looking for a more natural look and feel, stained is the way to go! Very similar to the painted decks are prepped, we sand and do a deep clean. Once the deck is ready, a stain of your choice is then applied. After about a day of the stain soaking into the wood, your natural and elegant looking deck is ready for use!

Deck Builds

Whether you want to fix the foundation on an existing deck, expand your deck, or just build a brand new one altogether, we do that too! From there we can discuss the type of deck and finish you desire!
